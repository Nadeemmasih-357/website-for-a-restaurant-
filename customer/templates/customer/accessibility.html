Accessibility

Accessibility Statement

RestaurantSite is committed to providing a website that is accessible to the widest possible audience, regardless of technology or ability. We strive to adhere to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) and other accessibility standards.

Accessible Design

We have designed our website with accessibility in mind, implementing features such as:

  • Use of semantic HTML elements
  • Proper labeling of form fields
  • Keyboard navigation support
  • Text alternatives for non-text content
  • Color contrast considerations

Feedback

We welcome feedback on the accessibility of our website. If you encounter any accessibility barriers or have suggestions for improvement, please let us know by contacting us at [contact@example.com].

Accessibility Tools

Below are some tools and resources that may assist users in accessing our website:

  • Screen readers: Software programs that read aloud text displayed on the screen, such as NVDA, JAWS, and VoiceOver.
  • Browser extensions: Extensions like WAVE, Axe, and HTML_CodeSniffer can help identify accessibility issues on web pages.
  • Zoom and magnification tools: Built-in browser features or third-party tools that allow users to zoom in on content for better readability.

For further assistance or inquiries regarding accessibility, please contact us at [contact@example.com].
